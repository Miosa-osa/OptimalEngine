  closed items.

## Decision log rules

Every decision goes into `signals/YYYY-MM-DD-decision-*.md` with:
- what was decided,
- who decided,
- rationale (one sentence),
- revisit date.

No verbal-only decisions. If it isn't in the workspace, it didn't happen.
