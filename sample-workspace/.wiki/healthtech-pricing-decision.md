## Summary

Healthtech partner pricing set at **$2K per seat** for Q4, conditional
on a three-year commitment with annual price protection. Anchored to
the customer's incumbent vendor at $2,400/seat — we underprice
deliberately to win the three-year lock-in
{{cite: optimal://nodes/04-academy/signals/2026-04-11-customer-pricing-call.md}}.

## Decision

- **Price**: $2,000 per seat per year.
- **Term**: 3 years with annual price protection.
- **Revenue recognition**: front-load one quarter on contract signing.
- **Volume at this pricing**: forecast 500 seats at go-live; $1M ARR.

## Why $2K

- Customer's anchor is $2,400 (incumbent vendor, narrower feature
  set) — $2K is recognizably cheaper without signaling "we're
  desperate".
- Three-year commitment secures budget certainty through 2028.
- Infra cost projection (microVM × seat × session) supports margin at
  this price {{cite: optimal://nodes/02-platform/signals/2026-04-10-microvm-spec.md}}.
